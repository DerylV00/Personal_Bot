04 · Operating Rules

Strategic Principles

Decisions made in advance so you don't have to make them under pressure. These are the rules of the Deryl VanNostrand playbook.

01
Build the Brand Before You Need It

You are a Blaze Genius — the brand IS you. Every post, every conversation, every event you show up to compounds into reputation capital. The mayor campaign in 2028–2030 succeeds or fails based on relationships built today. Start documenting your vision now — not when it's polished, but when it's real.

02
Hire Your Opposite Genius Early

Your losing formula is calculation — systems, spreadsheets, details. Your first hire or partner should be a Steel Genius. Property management, bookkeeping, and compliance must not live in your head. Free yourself to magnify. You are the fire; find someone to tend the infrastructure.

03
Real Estate Is the Vehicle, Community Is the Purpose

The STR portfolio is not the goal — it is how you finance the goal. Every property should be an experience that connects guests to Lake Ozark and your vision. Your properties should tell your WHY before you say a word. Short-term rentals in an experience-economy market are the perfect intersection of your skills and your philosophy.

04
Bitcoin Is Insurance, Not Speculation

Accumulate Bitcoin as a base layer of decentralized wealth — slow, steady, non-traded. This is not a get-rich play. It is alignment with your philosophy of decentralization and sovereignty. Hold through volatility. Never sell for lifestyle expenses. This is the money that outlasts every system you don't control.

05
Politics Flows From Reputation, Not Resume

In a small market like Lake Ozark, elections are won through relationships and trust accumulated over years. Show up to city council meetings. Know the business owners. Be the person people call when something needs to happen. The campaign announces what is already true — not a new identity created for an election.

06
The Body Is Part of the Brand

As a Blaze Genius and Gene Key 13 guide, you embody what you teach. Your physical transformation is not vanity — it is integrity. The man who coaches youth wrestlers and talks about discipline must live it. The body is a public commitment and the most visible proof of your philosophy in action.

07
Never Confuse Activity With Flow

Blaze Geniuses get distracted quickly — this is your named losing pattern in your own documents. When something stops feeling like flow, pause before pivoting. Ask: is this resistance (push through) or misalignment (course correct)? They feel identical at first. The answer is usually found in whether the action serves your WHY.

08
Partner Before You Scale

Your Gene Keys Pearl sequence says you thrive best in partnership. Before adding a third property, a new business, or a political campaign — find the right partner for that chapter. The Blaze who tries to do everything alone burns out before the goal. Your chaos shadow activates when you're operating without the right complement.

06 · Identity

Identity Commitments

Strategy follows identity. These are not goals — they are declarations of the person this plan assumes you already are. Read them like truths, not aspirations.

I am a connector.

I walk into every room asking who needs to meet whom. My network is my net worth and I tend it with the same discipline I apply to my body. Relationships are not networking — they are the mission itself.

I am a guide.

My Gene Keys Life's Work is to help others see the hero in their own story. Whether coaching wrestling, hosting a dinner, or running for office — I create spaces where people discover their own power. I never take credit for their transformation.

I am a builder of real things.

I invest in land, in community, in bodies, in relationships — not in abstractions. Everything I create can be touched, experienced, or inhabited. Real estate is real. Community is real. The body is real. The work must be real.

I am the face of this vision.

I do not hide behind a business or a brand. My name, my story, my transformation is the brand. This requires me to be consistent, visible, and unafraid. The Blaze who shrinks from the spotlight extinguishes the very thing that makes them powerful.

I am financially sovereign.

I build assets that generate income without my direct labor. Bitcoin. STR properties. Equity. I do not confuse income with wealth. I trade time for money only while building systems that will not require that trade. Sovereignty is the goal — not a number.

I am a man of my community.

I was planted in Lake Ozark, Missouri for a reason. Before I build nationally or globally, I plant deeply here. My community is my laboratory, my constituency, and my anchor. Missouri is the heart of America. This is where the work begins.

I commit.

I named myself "Emerging" — knowing what I want but not yet committed. This document is the commitment. The plan is made. The identity is chosen. The Chaos shadow of my Gene Keys is real — but so is the Innovation gift that lives on the other side of it. Now the work begins.
