Start With Why — Simon Sinek
The Golden Circle
WHY → HOW → WHAT. Most communicate outside-in. Inspiring leaders communicate inside-out. The WHY is the only thing that creates genuine loyalty.
The Golden Circle: WHAT (the outside) is what every organization can describe — what they sell, what they do. HOW (the middle) is how they do it — their process, their differentiator. For you: When you tell someone at the bar about real estate, don't start with your license or your brokerage. Start with: "I believe the strongest communities are built when people own real things — land, homes, food sources." Watch what happens. The right people lean in.
